## Death Before Age 75

<Callout type="info" title="Tax-Free Inheritance">
If you die before 75 and benefits are paid within 2 years:
- Lump sum: **Tax-free**
- Drawdown: **Tax-free withdrawals**
- Annuity purchase: **Tax-free income**

This applies to defined contribution pensions (workplace DC, SIPPs, personal pensions).
</Callout>

## Your Nomination Form

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical: Update Your Nomination">
Your pension doesn't automatically pass via your will. You must complete a **nomination form** with your pension provider naming your beneficiaries.

**Without a nomination:**
- Provider decides who inherits (discretionary)
- Could go to people you didn't intend
- Delays in payment
</Callout>
