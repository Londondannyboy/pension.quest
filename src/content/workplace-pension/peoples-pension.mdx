## Managing Your Account

### Online Access

Visit **thepeoplespension.co.uk** to:
- Check your pot value
- View contribution history
- Change investment choices
- Update personal details
- Download statements
- Nominate beneficiaries

<Callout type="tip" title="First Login">
You'll need your member number (from your welcome letter) and National Insurance number to register. You'll then create your own password.
</Callout>

## Transferring

### Transferring In

You can consolidate old pensions into The People's Pension:
- Request a transfer through your online account
- Provide details of your old pension
- The People's Pension handles the paperwork

### Transferring Out

You can transfer your People's Pension to another provider:
- No exit fees
- Request transfer value online
- New provider arranges the transfer
