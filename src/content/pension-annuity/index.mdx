A **pension annuity** gives you guaranteed income for life in exchange for your pension pot. It's one of the main ways to turn your savings into retirement income.

## What is an Annuity?

An annuity is an insurance product. You give an insurance company your pension pot, and they promise to pay you a fixed income for the rest of your life - no matter how long you live.

## How to Buy an Annuity

1. **Get multiple quotes** - Use comparison services or a broker
2. **Check for enhanced rates** - Disclose all health conditions
3. **Choose your options** - Level/escalating, single/joint, guarantee period
4. **Shop around** - Rates vary 10-20% between providers
5. **Consider advice** - For larger pots, advice may be worthwhile
