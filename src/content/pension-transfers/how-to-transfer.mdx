## Step 4: Request the Transfer

<Callout type="info" title="You Do This Through Your NEW Provider">
The new provider handles contacting your old provider. You don't need to phone or write to your old pension.

Most providers have an online transfer request process. You'll need:
- Old provider name
- Policy number
- Approximate value
</Callout>
