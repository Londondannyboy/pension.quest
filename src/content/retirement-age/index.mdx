## The Gap Between Private and State Pension

<Callout type="example" title="Planning for the Gap">
**Tom**, born 1965, plans to retire at 57:

- **Private pension access**: Age 57 (2028 rules)
- **State Pension age**: 67
- **Gap to bridge**: 10 years

Tom needs his private pension to cover 10 years before State Pension kicks in.
</Callout>
