You **can** opt out of your workplace pension, but should you? Here's what you need to know.

## How to Opt Out

### The Process

1. **Get the opt-out form** from your pension provider (details in your enrolment letter)
2. **Complete the form** with your personal details
3. **Submit within 1 month** of being enrolled for a full refund
4. **Receive confirmation** from your employer

<Callout type="warning" title="1 Month Deadline">
To get a full refund of any contributions already taken, you must opt out within **1 calendar month** of your automatic enrolment date.
</Callout>

## Re-Enrolment

If you opt out, your employer must re-enrol you:

- **Every 3 years** from the original staging date
- If you still meet eligibility criteria
- You can opt out again each time

<Callout type="info" title="Automatic Re-Enrolment">
You'll receive a letter when you're re-enrolled. You'll need to actively opt out again if you don't want to participate.
</Callout>
