**NEST** (National Employment Savings Trust) is the UK's largest workplace pension scheme, set up by the government to support auto-enrolment.

## What is NEST?

NEST is a **master trust pension scheme** created by the government to ensure all employers have access to a low-cost pension for auto-enrolment.

## Managing Your NEST Account

### Online Access

You can manage your NEST pension at **www.nestpensions.org.uk**:

- View your pot value and contributions
- Update personal details
- Change investment choices
- Nominate beneficiaries
- Download statements

<Callout type="info" title="First Time Login">
You'll receive login details by post when enrolled. You'll need your National Insurance number and the activation key from your welcome letter.
</Callout>
