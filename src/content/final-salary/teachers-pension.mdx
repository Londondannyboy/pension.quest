## Phased Retirement

<Callout type="info" title="Take Pension While Working">
Teachers can use phased retirement to:
- Take a portion of their pension (usually 75%)
- Continue working (reduced hours or same hours)
- Build up more pension entitlement

This can help transition to full retirement or manage workload.
</Callout>
