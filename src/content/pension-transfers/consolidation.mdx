## When NOT to Consolidate

<Callout type="warning" title="Don't Consolidate If">
- **Guaranteed annuity rates (GARs):** Could be worth thousands
- **Protected tax-free cash:** Some old pensions offer >25%
- **DB pensions:** Rarely makes sense to transfer out
- **Protected pension age:** May have to wait longer
- **With-profits guarantees:** Could lose bonuses
- **High exit fees:** May not be worth the cost
</Callout>
