## Investment Approach

NOW: Pensions uses a **diversified growth fund** that:
- Invests globally across different asset types
- Automatically adjusts risk as you approach retirement
- Aims for steady, long-term growth

<Callout type="info" title="Simple Investing">
Unlike some providers with multiple fund choices, NOW: Pensions focuses on one well-diversified default fund. This keeps things simple for most members.
</Callout>
