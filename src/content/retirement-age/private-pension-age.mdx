## Protected Pension Ages

Some people can still access their pension before 57:

<Callout type="info" title="Who Has Protection?">
**Protected pension age** applies if:

- Your scheme allowed access before 55 on 4 April 2006
- You joined that scheme before 6 April 2006
- You haven't transferred to a new scheme

Common examples: Some older defined benefit schemes, certain public sector schemes.
</Callout>

<Callout type="warning" title="Planning to Retire at 55?">
If you want to access your pension at 55, you must:
1. Be aged 55 before 6 April 2028
2. Start accessing your pension before that date

If you turn 55 after 5 April 2028, you'll need to wait until 57.
</Callout>

## Accessing Before Age 55

<Callout type="warning" title="Pension Scam Warning">
Anyone offering to help you access your pension before 55 (without a protected age) is likely a scammer. You could:

- Lose your entire pension
- Face tax charges up to 55%
- Have no recourse to recover funds

**Only access pensions through legitimate channels.**
</Callout>
