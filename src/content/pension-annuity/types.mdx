<Callout type="example" title="Level vs Escalating">
**£100,000 pot at age 65:**

**Level annuity:**
- Year 1: £6,500
- Year 10: £6,500
- Year 20: £6,500

**3% escalating:**
- Year 1: £4,600
- Year 10: £6,000
- Year 20: £8,100

Escalating catches up around year 12-15.
</Callout>
