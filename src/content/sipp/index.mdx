A **SIPP** (Self-Invested Personal Pension) gives you control over how your pension is invested. This guide explains everything you need to know.

## What is a SIPP?

A SIPP is a type of personal pension that allows you to:
- **Choose your own investments** from a wide range of options
- **Manage your portfolio** actively or passively
- **Access the same tax benefits** as other pensions

### 3. Investments Grow Tax-Free

Inside your SIPP:
- **No Capital Gains Tax** on growth
- **No dividend tax** on income
- **No Income Tax** until you withdraw

### 4. Access at Retirement

From age 55 (57 from 2028), you can:
- Take 25% tax-free
- Draw income (drawdown)
- Buy an annuity
- Take cash lump sums

## How to Open a SIPP

1. **Choose a provider** - Compare charges and investment options
2. **Apply online** - Usually takes 10-15 minutes
3. **Verify identity** - Upload ID documents
4. **Fund your account** - Set up contributions or transfer existing pensions
5. **Choose investments** - Select funds or build a portfolio
